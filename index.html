Modern Slang I Wish Would Go Away

Slang: six seven

Definition: A slang term often used online or in casual conversations as a joke or insult, usually implying something is dumb, pointless, or not worth taking seriously.

Why I Don't Like It: I don’t like this slang because it feels confusing and unnecessary. Many people don’t even know what it means, so it can make conversations harder to understand. Instead of clearly explaining an opinion, people use “67” in a dismissive way, which can come across as immature rather than funny.
